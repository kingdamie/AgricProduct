AgriPredict Documentation

Getting Started

To start using AgriPredict, create an account or log in if you already have one. Once logged in, you can access the prediction tool from your dashboard.

Making Predictions

To get a prediction:

  1. Navigate to the "New Prediction" page
  2. Fill in all required fields:
    • Crop: The type of crop you're analyzing (e.g., Maize, Rice, Yam, etc.)
    • Country: Where the crop is grown
    • Year: The year of interest
    • Area Harvested: Total area in hectares
    • Rainfall: Average rainfall in mm
    • Temperature: Average temperature in Â°C
    • Policy Flag: Current agricultural policy (e.g., Subsidy, Tariff, None)
    • Transport Cost: Cost to transport goods in USD (Cost to move the produce per tonne)
    • Demand Supply Gap: Difference between demand and supply
  3. Click "Predict" to see your results
Understanding Results

The prediction results will show three key metrics:

  • Predicted Production: Estimated total crop production in tonnes
  • Predicted Yield: Estimated yield per hectare in kg/ha
  • Predicted Price: Estimated market price in USD/tonne
Managing Your Data

All your predictions are saved in your dashboard where you can:

  • View historical predictions
  • See detailed results
  • Export data to CSV or JSON
  • Delete outdated predictions
Note on Accuracy

Predictions are based on statistical models and historical data. While we strive for accuracy, actual results may vary due to unforeseen environmental or economic factors.
